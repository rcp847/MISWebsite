The cow at the zoo

Today I went to the zoo . I saw a cow jumping up and down in a tree . He ran through a large tunnel that led to an abandoned house. I got hungry so I decided to get some peanuts I saw the cow again, but this time riding a large tricycle. After seeing the cow for the second time, I realized I was still hungry and wanted to get a snack. I got a large scoop of ice cream and it made me feel full. Afterwards I had to chase our bus to go back home. When I got home I told my mom about the cow riding the large tricycle at the zoo she did not believe me, but yet again who would believe you if you told them that story.
